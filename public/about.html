About NativeWrite

Your voice, elevated – not replaced.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves to write with confidence, no matter where they learned English. NativeWrite was born to help non‑native English speakers express themselves naturally, accurately, and authentically, without erasing their unique voice.

What We Do

NativeWrite is a smart text‑transformation tool that detects unnatural phrasing, word‑order errors, and subtle grammar mistakes that typical checkers miss. It then rewrites your text to sound like a native English speaker, while preserving your original meaning, tone, and personality.

Why We’re Different

  • No sign‑up, no data retention – your privacy comes first.
  • Dialect‑aware – choose US, UK, AU, CA, or let us auto‑detect.
  • Domain‑specific – academic, business, creative, or general.
  • Free to start – 4 transformations per day, up to 800 words each.
  • Respects your voice – we don’t “AI‑wash” your style; we polish it.

Who It’s For

Students writing theses and essays, researchers polishing papers, professionals drafting emails and reports, bloggers, and anyone who wants their English to be clear, natural, and confident.
