Frequently Asked Questions

Is IdiomOptima really free?

Yes. The free tier gives you 4 transformations per day, each up to 800 words. No credit card required. Upgraded plans (with higher limits) will be available later.

Do you store my text?

No. Your text is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing and is not stored on our servers. Logs are anonymised. You can read our full Privacy Policy.

How does it differ from Grammarly or QuillBot?

Grammarly focuses on grammar and style rules; QuillBot paraphrases. IdiomOptima specifically targets non‑native fluency. It rephrases awkward sentences to sound natural while keeping your original meaning and voice. It also respects your chosen dialect (US/UK/AU/CA) and domain (academic, business, creative).

What file types can I upload?

Currently we support DOCX (Word) and PDF uploads. Plain text can be pasted directly. Footnotes and superscripts are preserved.

Can I edit the suggestions myself?

Absolutely. After transformation, you can click any sentence to swap between the original and the nativised version. Or double‑click a sentence to edit it inline – your changes will be saved in the result panel.

Why do I sometimes see “daily limit reached”?

The free tier allows 4 transformations per day (per browser, using local storage). This resets every 24 hours. If you need more, you can upgrade later or wait for the next day.

Which dialects are supported?

US, UK, Australian, and Canadian English. You can choose manually or let the app auto‑detect the most appropriate dialect for your text.

Is the AI always correct?

No AI is perfect. While IdiomOptima uses a powerful Gemini model, we recommend you review the output. Our goal is to help, not replace your own judgment.

Do I need an account?

Not yet. The app works without any sign‑up. In the future, optional accounts will be available for premium features (unlimited transformations, history sync, etc.).

How can I contact support or give feedback?

Email us at contact@IdiomOptima.ai. We’d love to hear your thoughts and suggestions.
