IdiomOptima – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

IdiomOptima (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Text you submit: The original text you paste or upload is sent to our AI provider (Google Gemini API) solely to generate a transformed version. We do not store your text after processing, except for temporary transmission.
  • Usage data: Daily transformation count (stored locally in your browser via localStorage). This helps enforce the free daily limit.
  • Consent preference: Your acceptance of our Terms & Privacy is stored locally (localStorage).
  • Analytics: We use Microsoft Clarity and Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors use the App (e.g., page views, button clicks, session duration). These services collect anonymised data such as approximate location, device type, and browser. No personal identification is shared.
  • Chat widget: We use Tawk.to for live chat. When you initiate a chat, Tawk.to may collect your IP address, browser info, and the messages you send. Their privacy policy applies: tawk.to/privacy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide text transformation services (submitted text is processed in real‑time and not retained).
  • To improve the App’s performance and user experience (via analytics).
  • To enforce daily usage limits and remember your legal consent.
  • To respond to support inquiries via chat or email.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only as necessary to operate the App:

  • Google Gemini API: Your text is sent to Google’s AI service for transformation. Google’s privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Vercel: Hosting and analytics. Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Analytics and heatmaps. Microsoft Privacy Statement.
  • Tawk.to: Chat widget. Tawk.to Privacy.

4. Data Retention

  • Submitted text is not retained after the transformation response is delivered.
  • LocalStorage data (usage count, consent) remains on your device until you clear your browser data.
  • Analytics data is retained by Microsoft Clarity and Vercel according to their policies (typically 12–24 months).
  • Chat transcripts are stored by Tawk.to as per their retention schedule.

5. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Since we collect very little personal data and do not store submitted text, most requests would apply to:

  • Your chat messages (via Tawk.to) – you can contact us to request deletion.
  • Analytics data (anonymised, cannot be individually identified).

To exercise your rights, email contact@IdiomOptima.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your consent status and daily usage count. These are not shared with third parties. Third‑party services (Clarity, Tawk.to) may set their own cookies for analytics and chat functionality. You can block cookies via your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to remove it.

8. Security

We use industry‑standard measures (HTTPS, secure API calls) to protect data in transit. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure; you use the App at your own risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: contact@IdiomOptima.com
