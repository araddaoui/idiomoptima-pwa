NativeWrite – Privacy & Security

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Owner: Ali Raddaoui (sole proprietor)

1. Data We Collect

  • Text you submit: The text you paste or upload is sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. It is not stored on our servers after the transformation is complete.
  • Usage data: We use Vercel Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., page views, clicks, session recordings). This helps improve the App.
  • Local storage: Your daily transformation count is stored in your browser’s local storage (not on our servers).
  • No personal accounts: We do not ask for names, emails, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via the footer email link.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the AI transformation service.
  • To enforce daily usage limits (using local storage).
  • To analyze aggregate traffic and user behavior via analytics tools.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • Google Gemini API: Your input text is transmitted to Google’s servers to generate suggestions. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for how they handle data.
  • Vercel: Hosting provider – may log standard server data (IP addresses, request times) as part of normal operation. See Vercel’s Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records anonymized session replays and heatmaps. See Clarity’s Terms.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

4. Data Retention

We do not retain the text you submit. Logs from Vercel and Clarity are retained according to their respective policies. Local storage data stays on your device until you clear it.

5. Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all communications. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. You use the App at your own risk.

6. Children’s Privacy

NativeWrite is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Choices

  • You can clear your daily usage counter by clearing your browser’s local storage.
  • You can disable analytics by using browser extensions or private browsing mode.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

If the owner has no permanent residence, the laws of the United Arab Emirates (where the owner is a citizen) shall apply.
